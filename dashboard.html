Marketing Dashboard

This dashboard is a sample marketing performance overview built for my portfolio. It shows the kind of information a marketing team might look at when reviewing website and campaign performance.

Main KPIs

Total users, sessions, pageviews, engagement rate, conversions, and conversion rate.

What I would look for

I would check which traffic sources bring the most users and compare that to the channels driving conversions.

Why it matters

A dashboard helps turn marketing activity into something easier to explain, compare, and improve.
