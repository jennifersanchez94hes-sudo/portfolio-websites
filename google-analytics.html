Google Analytics

My Google Analytics work helped me understand how websites are measured and how marketers can use data to make smarter decisions.

What I learned

  • How GA4 organizes users, sessions, events, and conversions.
  • How reports and explorations can answer different marketing questions.
  • Why attribution, traffic sources, and funnel steps matter for campaign analysis.

How I would use it

I would use GA4 to see where visitors come from, what pages they interact with, and where people drop off before converting. This helps marketers decide what content, ads, or landing pages need improvement.

View GA4 Reflection

One takeaway from GA4 is that data is most useful when it connects back to a real marketing question.
